About SmartStay

SmartStay is an intelligent PG management platform designed to simplify accommodation for students, working professionals, and PG owners. Our system connects tenants with verified PGs, enabling seamless room booking, secure online payments, and smart communication in one unified portal.

With SmartStay, property owners gain real-time control over bookings, tenant records, and finance tracking, while tenants enjoy transparency, safety, and comfort throughout their stay.
